You Already Have Everything You Need

AI functions and capabilities are based upon human reasoning and mathematics. We can leave the mathematics to AI, and keep the deeper reasoning for ourselves.

Does AI reason like a human being?

AI is a model/simulation of how humans learn and think. It is a mirror for human reason. It “reasons” very fast, but at a shallower level than humans. AI lacks freedom, true imagination, and the rich integration of a living brain.

Does AI know and understand?

AI does not truly know or understand — it predicts. The quality of its answers depends 100% on the quality of your prompts.

You are the one who makes AI useful.
