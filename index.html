You Already Have Everything You Need

AI functions and capabilities are based upon human reasoning and mathematics.
We can leave the mathematics to AI, and keep the reasoning for ourselves (human reasoning is deeper and contains many more layers than a machine in this respect).
Knowing how to reason systematically (i.e when solving problems), will be a great advantage to understand how AI works.

Does AI reason like a human being?

AI is a model/simulation of the way humans learn and think.

AI is a mirror for human reason, for the way humans/animals learn from experience, solve problems, verify or critically analyse/synthesise information.

Yes, AI "reasons" and much faster than humans, but at a lower level.
AI lacks freedom, imagination and real integration given by the living and breathing brain (i.e humans perception v/s sensation).

AI reasons by following instructions (algorithms), which have been designed by a human using reasoning as you experience it every day.
AI does not know neither understands, it predicts instead.

Does AI knows and Understand?

AI does not know, neither understands.
The quality of AI's outcomes is directly related to the quality of your prompts (i.e. questions, instructions and/or requests).

You are the one who makes AI to align to your goals by prompting

AI can store and retrieve information, can search for patterns (sound, text and images), can predict the likelihood that its generated answer is correct (by using statistics and probabilities)
