Interactive Prompt Engineering

Learn how to communicate effectively with AI through hands-on practice

RAG - "Use My Notes"

Provide the AI with your own information and ask it to use that as its main source.

Example: Upload your budget and preferences, then ask the AI to plan a vacation based on that information.

COT - "Show Your Work"

Ask the AI to think through a problem step-by-step to make its reasoning clear.

Example: "Calculate my vacation cost. First, list flight costs, then add hotel, then food expenses, then add it all up."

ReACT - "Go and Find Out"

The AI searches for the latest information it doesn't have.

Example: "Find me the best flight deals to Spain. First check airline websites, then look for travel advisories."

DSP - "Get to the Point"

Give the AI a very clear, narrow focus for quick, specific answers.

Example: "List beachfront hotels in Malaga with pools. Ignore any without free cancellation."

ToT - "Weigh the Options"

The AI explores multiple paths and compares them to find the best one.

Example: "Evaluate beach vs. city vs. mountain vacations for a family with teenagers, then recommend the best one."
