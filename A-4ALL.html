Mixture of Experts (MoE)

You don’t need to know everything.
You just need to know how to ask the right people.

What is MoE?

MoE is like having a team of specialists in your pocket. Instead of asking the AI one big hard question, you break the problem into pieces and let different “experts” help with each piece.

Old Way

“Tell me how to save money.”

→ You get a long list that’s hard to follow.

MoE Way

“Act as a budget coach → then as a cheap chef → then as a psychologist who understands why I spend too much.”

→ You get a plan that actually works for real life.
