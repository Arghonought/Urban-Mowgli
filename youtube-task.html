💻 1. Turning on & connecting

📌 Instruction: Turn on your computer → enter your password → connect to Wi‑Fi. Then open your Internet browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox).

🌍 2. Address bar & YouTube website

📌 Instruction: In your browser, find the address bar (not the search box). Type www.youtube.com and press Enter. Differentiate: address bar shows exact web address; search box is for searching words.

📺 3. Search YouTube for a known news channel

📌 Instruction: On YouTube, click inside the YouTube search box. Type sky news and press Enter. Click on any video from the official "Sky News" channel.

🔴 4. Understanding a YouTube channel & LIVE

📌 Instruction: Click on the channel name (Sky News). Look for the LIVE tab. Also find "Podcasts" and "Playlists". Explore the channel homepage.

🎓 5. Find educational content

📌 Instruction: In YouTube search box, type "settings for an android phone". Open one clip that you understand. Look at: channel name, number of views, how long ago it was published.

⏲️ 6. Using filters (last month)

📌 Instruction: After a search (e.g., "settings for android"), click the FILTER button on YouTube. Choose "Last month" to see recent clips.

⌨️ 7. Keyboard & mouse skills

📌 Instruction: Practice: type letters/numbers, use space bar, delete/backspace, Enter key, Shift, Caps Lock. Also double-click to open something, and try Ctrl + mouse wheel to zoom.
