MoE Strategy Guide

Using AI Intelligence Instead of Just Knowledge

What is MoE and Why It Matters

MoE = Mixture of Experts

MoE is a strategy where you break down a complex problem into smaller parts and use different "experts" (or AI personas) to solve each part.

Instead of asking AI one big question, you become the conductor who directs different experts to work together.

Intelligence vs. Knowledge

Traditional education often focuses on knowledge - memorizing facts and information. But in the real world, intelligence is more valuable - knowing how to find information, solve problems, and make decisions.

With AI, you don't need to know everything. You need to know how to ask the right questions and combine different perspectives.

Example: Traditional vs. MoE Approach

Traditional: "Tell me about budgeting"

MoE Approach: "Act as a financial planner to create a budget framework, then as a frugal shopper to find savings, then as a psychologist to address spending habits."

Applying MoE to Real-Life Problems

Finding a New Job

Experts needed: Career counselor, resume writer, industry specialist, interview coach

Each expert focuses on a different aspect of the job search process.

Budgeting & Saving Money

Experts needed: Financial planner, frugal shopper, meal planner, psychologist

Different perspectives on money management, spending habits, and practical savings.

Learning a New Skill

Experts needed: Subject expert, learning specialist, motivational coach, practical applier

Each helps with different aspects of the learning process.

Build Your Own MoE Prompt

Follow these steps to create a custom MoE prompt for your specific needs:

1

Define Your Main Goal

What complex problem do you want to solve?

2

Identify Your Experts

What different perspectives do you need? (Add at least 2)

3

Define the Analysis & Output

How should the experts work together and present the final result?
